About

I currently work as an airport screening officer and did various professional roles in the past however, none of them satisified my creative side. Enter UX Design. I enjoy this field as it builds on top of my natural creativity and artistry. I hope to combine all of my past experiences, business education alongside all of the new technological skill sets I am developing to solve design problems in the workforce. I am always learning and growing, and would love an opportunity to grow with you! Feel free to contact me.

A few points about me:

  • Upgraded web technology knowledge by completing a Coursera's Google UX Design Professional Certificate and taking a Web Development & Coding Specialization certificate in HTML, CSS and JavaScript.
  • Experience in research, user interviews, usability testing, creating user stories and epics, storyboarding, sketching, and wire framing.
  • Strong knowledge of end-to-end business processes and analyzing data through various techniques.
